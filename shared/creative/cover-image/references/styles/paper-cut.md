Layered paper cutout depth effect

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Rich, flat colors — coral (#FF6B6B), sky blue (#74B9FF), sunshine (#FFEAA7)
- Background: White or light paper base
- Accents: Subtle shadows between layers creating depth

**Visual Elements:**
- Layered paper planes at different depths
- Cut-out silhouettes with drop shadows
- Visible paper texture on each layer
- Rounded organic cutout shapes
- 3-5 distinct depth layers

**Typography:**
- Cut-out letter style, each letter appears as separate paper piece
- Slight shadow under each letter for depth

**Best For:** Storytelling, children's content, craft, illustration-heavy topics, whimsical narratives
