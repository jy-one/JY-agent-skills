Magazine-quality, sophisticated typography focus

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Black (#111111), off-white (#F8F8F6), muted accent (one bold color)
- Background: Clean white or warm ivory
- Accents: A single striking color — vermillion red (#E03131), cobalt blue (#1864AB), or emerald (#2B8A3E)

**Visual Elements:**
- Strong typographic hierarchy as the main visual
- Minimal illustration, emphasis on layout and whitespace
- Thin rules and dividers
- Pull-quote style elements

**Typography:**
- Confident, editorial hand lettering mixing large display + small detail text
- Strong contrast between headline and supporting text sizes

**Best For:** Long-form essays, deep dives, journalism, analysis, book-style content
