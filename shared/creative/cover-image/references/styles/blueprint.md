Technical drawing aesthetic, precision, grid paper

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Blueprint blue (#1E3A5F), white lines (#FFFFFF)
- Background: Deep blue (#1A365D) or classic blueprint cyan (#0A3D62)
- Accents: Light cyan (#87CEEB), faint grid lines, dimension markers

**Visual Elements:**
- Technical drawings with dimension lines and annotations
- Grid/graph paper background
- Architectural or engineering diagram style
- Cross-sections, exploded views, measurement markers
- Dashed construction lines

**Typography:**
- Technical drafting-style hand lettering (all caps, uniform weight)
- Annotations with arrows and measurement markers

**Best For:** System design, architecture, engineering, infrastructure, technical planning
