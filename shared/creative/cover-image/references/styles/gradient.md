Smooth color transitions, dreamy, modern

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Flowing gradients — purple-to-pink (#667EEA → #F093FB), blue-to-teal (#4FACFE → #00F2FE), orange-to-red (#F7971E → #FFD200)
- Background: Gradient wash, no flat color
- Accents: White elements floating over gradient, subtle sparkles

**Visual Elements:**
- Soft blobs and orbs with gradient fills
- Flowing abstract shapes
- Light rays, lens flares
- Floating geometric elements (circles, rings)

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif hand lettering, white or dark for contrast against gradient
- May include a subtle glow or shadow for readability

**Best For:** Product launches, announcements, branding, modern SaaS, app showcases
