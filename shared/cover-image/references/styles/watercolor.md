Soft washes, organic bleed, painterly

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Transparent washes — cerulean (#5B9BD5), rose (#E8A0BF), ochre (#D4A745)
- Background: Textured watercolor paper white with visible grain
- Accents: Concentrated pigment spots, splatter marks, bleed edges

**Visual Elements:**
- Wet-on-wet watercolor washes with organic bleeding edges
- Soft focus, no hard outlines
- Paint drips, water blooms, salt texture effects
- Loose brushwork, visible brush strokes

**Typography:**
- Fluid brush-lettering style, slightly imperfect and organic
- Text may interact with paint (letters bleeding at edges)

**Best For:** Art, culture, creative writing, poetry, emotional topics, beauty
