East Asian brush ink aesthetic (水墨风)

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Ink black (#1A1A1A) in varying dilutions (浓淡), dark gray washes
- Background: Rice paper white (#FAF8F0) or aged xuan paper (#F5ECD7)
- Accents: A single vermillion red seal mark (朱印), occasional indigo or pale green wash

**Visual Elements:**
- Brush ink strokes with visible texture (飞白, 枯笔)
- Mountains, bamboo, clouds, water — traditional ink painting subjects
- Seal stamp (印章) as accent element
- Ink splatter and drip marks for energy
- Generous empty space (留白) — emptiness is as important as the marks

**Typography:**
- Calligraphic brush-style lettering (行书 or 草书 style)
- May include a classical Chinese phrase alongside modern title
- Red seal stamp near text

**Best For:** Chinese culture, philosophy, calligraphy, traditional topics, 国风 content, meditation, wisdom
